Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

🔒 Short version: RateFlow does not collect your personal data, does not require registration, and does not sell information about you. This page explains exactly how the site works.

1. Who We Are

RateFlow is a free currency conversion tool available at rateflowcurrency.netlify.app. Contact: rateflowcurrency@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

Personal data: None. RateFlow does not ask for your name, email address, or any other personally identifying information. There is no account creation, no login, and no registration.

Analytics data: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns — which pages are visited, how long visitors spend, what country they visit from, and what device they use. This data is anonymised and aggregated. We cannot identify you individually.

Local storage: RateFlow stores exchange rate data, your theme preference, favorite pairs, and rate alerts in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3. Cookies

RateFlow itself does not set tracking cookies. However, third-party services embedded on the site may set their own cookies. See our Cookie Notice for the full breakdown.

4. Google AdSense

RateFlow displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out at Google's Ad Settings.

5. Affiliate Links

RateFlow contains affiliate links to third-party money transfer services. These are disclosed on pages where they appear. When you click an affiliate link and sign up, we may receive a commission. This comes at no additional cost to you and does not affect our editorial opinions.

6. The open.er-api.com API

Exchange rates are fetched from open.er-api.com. When your browser fetches a rate, a request is made to their servers. RateFlow does not pass any personal information in these requests — only currency codes.

7. Data Sharing

RateFlow does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only third parties that may receive data about your visit are Google (via AdSense and Analytics) and open.er-api.com (currency requests, no personal data).

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most GDPR and CCPA rights are satisfied by default. For data held by Google, exercise your rights through Google's own tools and privacy dashboard.

9. Children's Privacy

RateFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

11. Contact

Questions? Email rateflowcurrency@gmail.com.
