Western Union for Emergencies

Western Union is the fastest option for cash pickup emergencies because:

  • Transfers are typically available for pickup within minutes of sending online
  • Your recipient only needs their ID and your transfer number (MTCN) — no bank account, no phone
  • 500,000+ agent locations including hospitals, post offices, and banks in most cities
  • Available 24/7 at many agent locations

To send the fastest way: use westernunion.com or the app (not an agent branch, which takes longer). Pay with debit card for instant processing.

Mobile Money for Emergencies

If your recipient has an active MTN MoMo, M-Pesa, or Orange Money wallet, Remitly Express is often the fastest and cheapest option simultaneously — making it the best overall choice for emergencies in most of Africa:

  • Transfer initiated online in under 2 minutes
  • Arrives in the mobile wallet in under 5 minutes typically
  • Recipient can use the funds immediately — pay for medical services, buy food, or withdraw at any agent

Emergency Transfer Checklist

  • ✅ Confirm your recipient's details BEFORE starting (full name as on ID, phone number for mobile money, exact pickup country/city)
  • ✅ Have your own ID and payment method ready
  • ✅ Send online — faster than going to an agent branch
  • ✅ Pay with debit card — bank transfer takes longer to process
  • ✅ Share the MTCN / reference number with your recipient immediately after sending
  • ✅ Tell your recipient to go immediately — cash pickup amounts can sometimes be placed on hold if not collected within 24–48 hours if fraud checks trigger

What to Avoid in an Emergency

  • Your regular bank's wire transfer: Typically 1–5 business days. Not suitable for emergencies.
  • PayPal: International PayPal transfers can take days to clear and involve high fees.
  • Cryptocurrency: Unless your recipient is already set up and experienced, this adds complexity that slows things down.
  • Informal networks: Speed may seem attractive but there's no protection if something goes wrong.
